DAR ES SALAAM

Dar es Salaam: is the largest city and financial hub of Tanzania.
With a population of over five million people, Dar es Salaam is the largest city in East Africa and the sixth-largest in Africa.
Located on the Swahili coast, Dar es Salaam is an important economic center and one of the fastest-growing cities in the world.
